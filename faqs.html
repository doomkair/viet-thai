Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make a reservation for a large group or special occasion?

Absolutely! We gladly accept reservations for large groups and special occasions. Please contact our friendly staff in advance to secure your reservation and discuss any specific requirements.

Do you offer takeout or delivery services?

Yes, we offer convenient takeout and delivery services. You can enjoy our delicious Vietnamese and Thai cuisine from the comfort of your home. Check our website or give us a call to place your order.

Is your restaurant kid-friendly and are there specific menu options for children?

We welcome families with open arms! Our restaurant is kid-friendly, and we can design the food to please young palates, please clearly marked for our servant your kids' preferences.

Are there gluten-free or allergen-friendly options available?

Absolutely. We understand the importance of catering to various dietary needs. Our menu includes gluten-free and allergen-friendly options, clearly marked for your convenience. Inform our staff about any specific allergies or dietary restrictions, and we'll be happy to assist you.

Do you have parking facilities or recommendations for nearby parking?

We provide convenient on-site parking for our guests. In addition, we can offer recommendations for nearby parking options if our on-site spaces are occupied during busy periods. Feel free to inquire with our staff upon arrival.

What are the accepted payment methods at your restaurant?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including cash, major credit cards, and mobile payment platforms. You can enjoy a hassle-free dining experience without worrying about the payment method.

Can I bring my own drinks? Is there a corkage fee?

We welcome guests to bring their own drinks to accompany their meal. We do not charge a corkage fee for outside beverages. Feel free to bring your preferred drinks and enjoy them during your dining experience at our restaurant. However, we also offer a selection of beverages for purchase if you prefer to explore our menu.

Are there private dining or event spaces available for reservation?

Yes, we have private dining and event spaces available for reservation. Whether you're hosting a business gathering, a family celebration, or any special occasion, our dedicated event team will assist you in planning a memorable experience tailored to your needs. Contact us for more information and availability.
